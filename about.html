About Us

Welcome to Gamma Point

At Gamma Point, we believe every love story deserves to be beautifully told and timelessly preserved. Our passion is transforming your cherished wedding memories into exquisite, handcrafted albums that will be treasured for generations.

Our Story

Founded in 2000 in guindy, Gamma Point was born out of a deep appreciation for the art of photography and the magic of weddings. Our journey began with a simple mission: to create stunning wedding albums that capture the essence of each couple's unique love story. With over 24 years of experience, we have become a trusted name in the wedding industry, known for our exceptional craftsmanship and personalized service.

What We Do

We specialize in custom-designed wedding albums that are as unique as the couples we work with. Our process is collaborative and tailored to your needs, ensuring that every page of your album reflects your style and personality. From classic leather-bound albums to modern, minimalist designs, we offer a wide range of options to suit every taste.

Our Process

Consultation: We begin with a one-on-one consultation to understand your vision and preferences.

Design: Our talented designers create a bespoke album layout, incorporating your favorite photos and moments.

Review: You review the design and provide feedback to ensure it’s perfect.

Production: Once approved, our skilled craftsmen bring your album to life using high-quality materials and printing techniques.

Delivery: Your beautifully finished album is carefully packaged and delivered to your doorstep.

Why Choose Us?

Quality: We use only the finest materials and state-of-the-art printing technology to ensure your album is of the highest quality.

Personalization: Each album is uniquely designed to tell your love story, down to the smallest detail.

Experience: Our team of designers and craftsmen are experts in their field, dedicated to creating beautiful and lasting keepsakes.

Customer Service: We pride ourselves on providing exceptional service from start to finish, making the entire process seamless and enjoyable.

Meet the Team

Our team is a passionate group of designers, photographers, and craftsmen who are dedicated to creating the perfect wedding album for you. We combine artistic talent with technical expertise to deliver stunning results.
